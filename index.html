About this game

Penfight was invented in 18th century England. The legend has it Sir Allen Parker, the earl of Chelpark (ancestor of Camilla Parker Bowles) was in his study when an assasin broke into the room and tried to assassinate him. The earl was able to defend himself with just a pen and stabbed the guy with the nib. The earl then coined the phrase the pen is mightier than the sword. To commemorate the event he organized jousts using pens. This is the first ever penfight recorded in history.
